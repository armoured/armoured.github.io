A.L.I.C.E

A.L.I.C.E - an app to improve cyber security awareness

Nowadays cyber awareness is a necessity. I helped UNSW to build A.L.I.C.E, a retro style 2D platform side scrolling game, with enemies to zap, puzzles to solve, and an overall learning experience. Players choose their avatar, and the game begins with your identity being stolen. The player journeys through 5 levels, facing numerous cyber threats, and using their wits to solve cyber puzzles. A friendly A.I. (Artificial Intelligence) will help the player through the game, and provide tips and hints on how to defeat the final Cyber Threat boss, to retrieve their identity back.

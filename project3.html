EY P&U

EY P&U Blockchain Maintenance Cycle Asset Management System

The Compass program was an 8 week program where I worked alongside a group of students with mentors from EY. We developed a blockchain solution for P&U Maintenance Cycle Asset Management with the backend relying on the hyperledger framework and the front-end on react-js and node.js. The application of blockchain solves the problem of Point-to-Point systems, which create bottlenecks in the maintenance cycle because data never flows past one node. The various processes we identified in our system were Safety Observations, Maintenance Notifications, Maintenance Orders, Supplier Engagement, Work Order Completion and Issue Payments. Unfortunately, I cannot share the mvp we built.

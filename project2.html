S.I.R.I.U.S - Artificial Intelligence that provides instant feedback on course work

S.I.R.I.U.S was an ai that I developed as an MVP during my time in the UNSW Heroes program. S.I.R.I.U.S aims to provide students instantaneous feedback on their course work. By utilising machine learning and natural language processing, it takes a lecturers correct solution to a topic and breaks it down. When a student uploads their solution - S.I.R.I.U.S already has knowledge of the topic and is able to provide feedback on where the student may improve. It does this by examining the context of the solution and rating keywords on how relevant they are. The entire system utilises the IBM Watson framework.
